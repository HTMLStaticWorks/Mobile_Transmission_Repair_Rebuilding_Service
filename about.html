Our Story

Founded in 2010, ShiftFix started with a simple idea: transmission repair shouldn't be a headache. We realized that many people struggle with the logistics of getting their car to a shop for major transmission work.

Today, we operate a fleet of fully equipped mobile units capable of performing complex diagnostics and on-site repairs, backed by a state-of-the-art rebuilding facility for more intensive projects.

Our Technical Philosophy

We don't just fix gears; we optimize drivetrain performance.

Electronic Mastery

Modern transmissions are complex computers. Our technicians are trained to decode sensor data and reflash TCMs (Transmission Control Modules) to factory specs or better.

Fluid Engineering

Fluid is the lifeblood of your transmission. We perform molecular-level analysis of your old fluid to detect internal wear patterns before they lead to total failure.

Precision Rebuilds

Every rebuild happens in a clean-room environment. We replace all "soft" parts and upgrade "hard" parts to address known factory defects in specific models.
